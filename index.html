Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a limit to the number or size of PDFs I can add?

You can upload up to 50 PDF files at once, with a total maximum size of 100MB per batch. This ensures fast processing for all users.

Will the quality of my PDFs be reduced?

No. This is not a PDF compressor, but a PDF *archiver*. The ZIP compression applies to the container, not the PDFs inside. Your original files remain unchanged and at their original quality.

Are my files safe and private?

Extremely safe. The entire process is automated. Your files are never stored on our server for more than a few minutes and are permanently deleted immediately after you download your ZIP file or after a 1-hour timeout. They are never shared, viewed, or used for any purpose.

What does this tool actually do?

It takes all the PDF files you provide and packages them into a single, standard ZIP file. This makes them easier to manage, download, and share as a single unit.
